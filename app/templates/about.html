ENE Analytics

This page is an independent visualization of the data collected by ENE (Encuesta Nacional de Empleo, National Employment Survey) which is conducted in Chile since year 2010 by INE (Instituto Nacional de Estadísticas, National Statistics Institute).

INE made available raw anonymized data via Employment and Unemployment (Ocupación y desocupación), link "Bases de datos", and at the moment of writing the publicly available database contains data for moving quarters from January-March 2010 to February-April of 2020, which captures the beginning of COVID-19 pandemic consequences in Chile.

  • Workforce participation analysis

    • Workforce participation evolution, split by zone/region, age and gender

  • Unemployment analysis

    • Unemployment rate evolution, split by zone/region, age and gender
    • Unemployment rate by age groups, split by zone/region and gender
